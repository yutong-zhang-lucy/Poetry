# UX Design & Future Roadmap

## 1. Design Principles: "Modern Ink"
We prioritize a clean, distraction-free interface that respects Chinese aesthetic traditions:
* **Typography:** Support for vertical typesetting and high-contrast "Reading Modes."
* **Interactions:** Minimalist navigation focusing on the "Recitation Journey."

## 2. Success Metrics for V1.0
* **[ ] Universal Search:** Efficiently query by Title, Author, Dynasty, or specific lines.
* **[ ] User Retention:** Target users mastering at least 10 poems in the initial cycle.
* **[ ] Completion Rate:** Aim for a recitation success rate of >70%.

## 3. Future Roadmap (V2.0 & Beyond)
* **[ ] Intelligent Spaced Repetition:** Integration of the Ebbinghaus Forgetting Curve to trigger automated review reminders.
* **[ ] Smart Push:** Notifications based on calculated "Memory Strength" thresholds.
* **[ ] Aesthetic Cards:** One-click generation of poetry cards for social media sharing.
